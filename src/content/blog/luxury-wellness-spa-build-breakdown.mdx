When this luxury wellness spa came to us, they were running their business across five disconnected platforms. ClickFunnels at $127/month, Squarespace at $65/month, plus hours of manual work every week on social media and client follow-ups.

Here's exactly what we built and why.

## The website: 15 pages in Astro

We replaced the Squarespace site with a custom 15-page website built in Astro, the same framework powering this blog. The result:

- **$0/month hosting** on Cloudflare Pages (vs. $65/month on Squarespace)
- **Sub-second load times** thanks to static HTML with no server rendering
- **SEO-optimized** with proper meta tags, schema markup, and semantic HTML
- **Mobile-first** responsive design built for the luxury wellness audience

The site includes a main spa section, a corporate wellness sub-site with three service tiers, a membership portal ("Join the Tribe"), and an SEO blog, all under one domain.

## The automation: 11 workflows running 24/7

This is where the real value lives. Using n8n (self-hosted, ~$6/month), we built 11 automation workflows:

1. **Booking confirmation:** instant email + SMS when a client books
2. **24-hour follow-up:** automated check-in after appointments
3. **Review request:** timed request sent 2 days post-visit
4. **Lead nurturing:** 5-email sequence for new contacts
5. **Social publishing:** content queue across 4 platforms
6. **Daily dashboard:** Slack briefing every morning at 8 AM
7. **New lead alert:** instant Slack notification when a form is submitted
8. **Membership onboarding:** welcome sequence for new members
9. **Re-engagement:** automated outreach to clients inactive 30+ days
10. **Corporate wellness intake:** form submission to CRM + follow-up
11. **Weekly performance digest:** metrics summary every Monday

Each workflow was custom-designed for the client's specific business logic, not a template or preset.

## The dashboard: one place for everything

Every morning at 8 AM, a Slack message arrives with:

- Yesterday's bookings and revenue
- New leads and form submissions
- Social media engagement across all platforms
- Any items requiring attention or approval

No more logging into five different dashboards. No more wondering what happened while you slept. The business reports to you.
